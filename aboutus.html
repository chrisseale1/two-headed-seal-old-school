About Me

Former therapist changing careers, moving from the psychology world into the web design world after realizing web development is where my passion lies. I have a keen interest in continuing to learn and grown my knowledge base in multiple languages and frameworks for web development. I would like to bridge the gap between object-oriented and functional programming. Ultimately, the most important thing is what dazzles on the page. I am versed in languages like HTML, CSS/LESS/SASS, Javascript, Typescript, Jquery, etc. I tend to do work in frameworks like Angular, Wordpress, React, PlatypusTS, and more. In addition, I have an in depth knowledge of Photoshop, Illustrator, SQL, and a working knowledge of various other programs. Every day, I learn at least one new thing to help me along the way.
